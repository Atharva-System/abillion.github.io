How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and navigate to the Settings menu.
  2. Click on "Delete Account."
  3. Confirm your decision to delete your account. Please note that this action is irreversible and will permanently delete all of your data associated with the account.
  4. Once confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion. You may be logged out immediately or after a short period of time.
  5. If you have any remaining outstanding issues or concerns, feel free to contact our support team for assistance before proceeding with the deletion.

That's it! Your account will be deleted according to our deletion policy.
